<table>
<tr><td>

### 🧬 01 — ElasticNet Regression
**Fusion of L1 + L2 regularization**

$$
\hat{\beta} = \arg\min_{\beta}\left\{\frac{1}{2n}\sum_{i=1}^n (y_i - x_i^T\beta)^2 + \lambda\left[\alpha\|\beta\|_1 + \frac{1-\alpha}{2}\|\beta\|_2^2\right]\right\}
$$

</td></tr>
<tr><td>

### 🌀 02 — Taylor Series Expansion
**Approximating reality one derivative at a time**

$$
f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n
$$

</td></tr>
<tr><td>

### ⚡ 03 — Newton's Method
**Converging on truth through iteration**

$$
x_{n+1} = x_n - \frac{f(x_n)}{f'(x_n)}
$$

$$
\theta_{n+1} = \theta_n - H^{-1}(\theta_n)\,\nabla f(\theta_n)
$$

</td></tr>
<tr><td>

### 🔻 04 — Gradient Descent
**The path every neural network walks blind**

$$
\theta_{t+1} = \theta_t - \eta \nabla_\theta J(\theta)
$$

</td></tr>
<tr><td>

### 🧠 05 — Deep Learning Core (Forward + Backprop)
**Where the machine begins to feel its own weights**

$$
z^{[l]} = W^{[l]}a^{[l-1]} + b^{[l]}, \qquad a^{[l]} = g(z^{[l]})
$$

$$
\mathcal{L} = -\frac{1}{m}\sum_{i=1}^{m}\left[y_i\log(\hat{y}_i) + (1-y_i)\log(1-\hat{y}_i)\right]
$$

$$
\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial W^{[l]}} = \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial a^{[l]}}\cdot\frac{\partial a^{[l]}}{\partial z^{[l]}}\cdot\frac{\partial z^{[l]}}{\partial W^{[l]}}
$$

</td></tr>
<tr><td>

### 👁️ 06 — Sigmoid & Softmax
**The gates that decide what the machine believes**

$$
\sigma(x) = \frac{1}{1+e^{-x}} \qquad\qquad \text{softmax}(z_i) = \frac{e^{z_i}}{\sum_{j=1}^K e^{z_j}}
$$

</td></tr>
<tr><td>

### 🎲 07 — Bayes' Theorem
**Updating belief in the face of new evidence**

$$
P(A|B) = \frac{P(B|A)\,P(A)}{P(B)}
$$

</td></tr>
<tr><td>

### 🔮 08 — PCA / Eigen Decomposition
**Compressing reality without losing its soul**

$$
\Sigma v = \lambda v
$$

</td></tr>
<tr><td>

### ⚔️ 09 — Support Vector Machine
**Drawing the sharpest possible line between chaos and order**

$$
\min_{w,b}\ \frac{1}{2}\|w\|^2 + C\sum_{i=1}^n \xi_i \quad \text{s.t. } y_i(w^Tx_i+b)\ge 1-\xi_i
$$

</td></tr>
<tr><td>

### 🌌 10 — K-Means Clustering
**Finding order in the noise**

$$
J = \sum_{k=1}^{K}\sum_{x_i \in C_k}\|x_i - \mu_k\|^2
$$
